About Me

Senior Frontend Developer | UI Engineer

I’m Niroop Kumar, a Senior Frontend Developer and UI Engineer with 8+ years of overall professional experience, including 6+ years specializing in UI and frontend development, building scalable and user-focused web interfaces.

I specialize in transforming design concepts and UI mockups into pixel-perfect, production-ready interfaces, ensuring visual consistency, accessibility, and smooth user experiences across mobile, tablet, and desktop devices.

I have delivered and maintained 5,000+ high-quality marketing and campaign landing pages for global clients, with a strong focus on:

  • High UI quality and layout precision
  • Responsive, mobile-first design
  • Cross-browser compatibility (Chrome, Firefox, Safari)

My approach emphasizes clean, reusable UI components, structured CSS, and performance-optimized layouts. I collaborate closely with design, marketing, and QA teams in Agile environments to deliver visually engaging and functionally reliable interfaces.

Alongside strong UI expertise, I bring hands-on experience with React.js (functional components and internal UI modules) and a solid understanding of UI/UX best practices, usability principles, and design systems.

I’m passionate about crafting intuitive, consistent, and impactful digital experiences.
